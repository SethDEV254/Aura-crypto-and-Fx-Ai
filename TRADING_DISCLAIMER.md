## What Can Go Wrong

### Common Reasons Trades Fail
1. **Market Volatility**: Sudden price spikes or crashes
2. **News Events**: Unexpected announcements (FOMC, CPI, etc.)
3. **Liquidity Issues**: Slippage, gaps, stop loss hunting
4. **False Breakouts**: Price reverses after entry
5. **Whipsaw Markets**: Choppy, directionless movement
6. **Black Swan Events**: Unpredictable major events

### Example Scenarios

#### Scenario 1: High Confidence Trade Fails
```
AI Analysis: 90% Confidence, BULLISH
Entry: $77,200
Stop Loss: $76,800
Target: $78,000

Result: Price drops to $76,800, stop loss hit
Loss: -$400 per unit
Reason: Unexpected news caused market reversal
```

#### Scenario 2: Low Confidence Trade Wins
```
AI Analysis: 60% Confidence, BEARISH
Entry: $77,200
Stop Loss: $77,600
Target: $76,400

Result: Price reaches $76,400, target hit
Profit: +$800 per unit
Reason: Market followed technical analysis
```

**Lesson**: Confidence scores don't guarantee outcomes.

## Responsible Trading Guidelines

### Before You Trade
1. **Educate Yourself**: Learn technical analysis, risk management, and trading psychology
2. **Practice First**: Use demo accounts before risking real money
3. **Start Small**: Begin with minimum position sizes
4. **Have a Plan**: Know your entry, stop loss, and targets before entering
5. **Accept Losses**: Losses are part of trading, manage them properly

### During Trading
1. **Follow Your Plan**: Don't move stop losses or targets emotionally
2. **Use Stop Losses**: ALWAYS protect your capital
3. **Manage Position Size**: Risk only 1-2% per trade
4. **Stay Disciplined**: Don't revenge trade or overtrade
5. **Keep Records**: Track all trades for analysis

### After Trading
1. **Review Trades**: Learn from both wins and losses
2. **Adjust Strategy**: Improve based on results
3. **Manage Emotions**: Don't let wins or losses affect you
4. **Take Breaks**: Avoid burnout and overtrading
5. **Seek Help**: Consult professionals if struggling

- Trading without stop losses
- Checking trades constantly
